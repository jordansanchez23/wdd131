Titanes Club

titanes-club.com

Purpose

The site aims to provide information about Titanes' activities, including their regular season games, summer school programs, and a registration page for their annual summer tournament.

Scenarios

  • As a basketball fan, where can I find upcoming games and events organized by Titanes?
  • How can I register for Titane's summer camp and what are the requirements?
  • How can I register for Titanes' summer tournament and what are the requirements?
